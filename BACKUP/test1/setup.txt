Converting =}-[Linux]Kde neon -> [Linux]Dynamic-OS

1. Systemd Custom Service for Oscyra DM
File: /etc/systemd/system/oscyra-dm.service

[Unit]
Description=Oscyra Custom Display Manager
After=network.target systemd-user-sessions.service

[Service]
User=guest
Environment=DISPLAY=:0
ExecStart=/usr/bin/oscyra-session.sh
Restart=always

[Install]
WantedBy=graphical.target

2. copy oscyra files
	[Oscyra], [oscyra-session.sh], oscyra-installer]
	[oscyra-start], [oscyra-session.desktop], [oscyra-login]
      	move > /usr/bin/ <--here

3. Disable other DMs
	systemctl disable sddm
	systemctl stop sddm

4. Enable custom DM service
	systemctl daemon-reload
	systemctl enable oscyra-dm.service

5. edit tty1 for autologin
	create/edit > /etc/systemd/system/getty@tty1.service.d/override.conf
	#------------#
	#|[Service] |#
	#|ExecStart=|#-------------------------------------------------#
	#|ExecStart=-/sbin/agetty --autologin guest --noclear %I $TERM|#
	#--------------------------------------------------------------#


6. Check log for correct setup
	systemctl status oscyra-dm.service
	journalctl -u oscyra-dm.service -b


Quick validation:

Step 1: systemd service is correctly set, running custom script as user guest(default) on DISPLAY=:0.

Step 2: files copied to /usr/bin/ with proper permissions assumed.

Step 3: disables and stops sddm to prevent conflicts.

Step 4: reloads systemd and enables custom service.

Step 5: autologin on tty1 for guest ensures custom service starts automatically after boot/login.

Step 6: log checking step for troubleshooting.
